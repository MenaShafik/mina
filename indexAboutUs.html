Real estate is expanding in the Egyptian market day after day, despite the huge urban expansions taking place in all of Egypt's governorates. There is still great difficulty for the Egyptian citizen in obtaining an ideal housing unit at a reasonable price, whether for purchase or rent.

Therefore, we designed and developed the platform to achieve a balance between what the buyer requires and what is available in the current market away from the eyes of the real estate broker, through a powerful search engine that enables the buyer to specify all the required specifications in the property, and then a quick filtering process begins for the contents of the site and offering all properties that meet the required specifications, in order for the researcher to have the freedom and flexibility to choose from among the available options.

It is worth noting that this advanced search process provides you with a comprehensive view of real estate prices currently in circulation, as well as getting acquainted with the general specifications of residential, commercial or administrative units in the chosen area.
